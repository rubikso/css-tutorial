It took me quite some time to learn how to do this and get to a point where I could do it with minimal referencing the video/blog. I spent far more hours getting the proccess down than I would have liked...(somewhere upwards of 20 hours from when I started). It took me so long because I spent a while watching the video, reading her blog article about it, and copying what I was seeing. Then I attemtped to recreate the design, but specific to me. That didnt turn out very well to say the least... But I perservered, and tried again, and this time I am proud to say I got the general idea down and manaaged to re-create the Dropdown Navigation Menu with only a few references to the original (just to work out some kinks towards the end)
